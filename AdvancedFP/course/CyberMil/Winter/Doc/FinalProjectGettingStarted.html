Getting Started on the Final Project

The final project is a group project. You have 15 days to prepare your powerpoint and oral presentation. Organization is the key to a good job.

  • Goal - work in small groups
    1. Study the group grading policy
    2. Think about your own performance in past groups.
    3. What would you like to see in this group?
  • Key - Get Organized
    1. Create a group list. Names, emails, phone numbers at a minimum.
    2. Create a calendar of when the group will meet.
    3. Find places to meet.
    4. Make a group inventory of who knows what
  • People have different roles
    1. scribe
    2. word smith
    3. researcher
    4. reader
    5. spokesperson
    6. Powerpoint expert
    7. News researcher
    8. Ethics researcher
  • Everyone should participate
    1. Keep track of attendance
    2. Assign everyone a speaking part in the presentation
    3. Practice the presentation with an audience.
