Good and Bad Enablings

Here is a list of positive and negative enablings that technology provides in the sphere of interpersonal relationships. We constructed this list in a class discussion. Thanks to David L. for taking these notes.

What are some good enablings?

  • The Deaf, communcation disorders
  • Reunions, find old classmates
  • Nobody knows your ugly, old
  • Pathologicaly shy
  • Housebound
  • Disease support groups
  • Dating and match making
  • Keeping in touch with people you don't see face to face
  • telepresence
  • Contact anywhere anytime
  • Anonymity - Whistle blowers, speaking truth to power.
  • music sharing

What are some bad enablings?

  • Narcism
  • stalking/ ease of finding address, phone numbers,schedule
  • Internet bullying
  • on-line affairs
  • ignoring actual friends for on-line friends
  • sexual predators
  • isolation in the real world
  • Anonymity - Lies and Deception, people take less responsibility
  • People don't self-censor their thoughts
