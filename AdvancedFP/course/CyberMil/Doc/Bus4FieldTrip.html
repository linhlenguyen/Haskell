How to get to the Memorial Plaza by bus

According to the Trimet trip planner the best way to get to the memorial by bus from our classroom is as follows.

  1. SW 6th & Market (Stop ID 7772)
  2. 12:42 p.m. Board 19 Glisan to Gateway TC See footnote
  3. 12:48 p.m. Get off at W Burnside & SW 2nd
  4. Walk 0.2 mile northeast to NW Couch St & NW Naito Pkwy
  5. Travel time: 16 minutes (including 10 minutes walking)
