My Poem

I am Husband, Father, Brother, Son.
I am Teacher, Mathematician, Scholar.
I am my Father's son, 12 years after his death.
I am my mother's caregiver, even when she doesn't remember my name.
I am one of 42 first cousins.
I am the peacemaker in my extended family.
I am dog-walker, pilot, swimmer.
I am sometimes lonely, sometimes too busy, sometimes overwhelmed.
