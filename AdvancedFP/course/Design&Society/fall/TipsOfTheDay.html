Tips of the Day
  1. Get you Odin account in the basement of Smith Hall.
  2. Buy lunch from the Indian Cart at 4th and Hall (Across from the Engineering Building)
  3. PSU students get in free at the 5th Ave cinema.
  4. Check the availabilty of labs using the link http://www.oit.pdx.edu/irs/tl/services.phtml
  5. PSU sells more parking permits than there are spaces. Get here to early to get a space.
  6. The Writing Center has drop in and appointments. They have Friday workshop times, 11:00am - 1:00pm in Cramer 101.
  7. PSU football games are free to PSU students.
  8. The writing center has a grammar workshop for speakers of a foreign language. Every Monday from 12:30 - 2:00.
  9. Tutoring for math courses on the 3rd floor of Neuberger Hall. Tutoring is done by math grad students.
  10. The Student Parent Center ( http://www.sps.pdx.edu/ ) provides support for students who are also parents.
