Case Study Analysis

In this assignment each group will read one of the case studies A1-A5 or B1-B4. You will work in groups of 3. You will choose a case study in Mentor section. No group should do the same case study.

  • Read the chosen case study and form your initial answers to the questions at the bottom of the case.

  • Discuss the answers with your group.

  • Who are the stakeholders in this situation? (You will need to have read Chapter 2 of Weiss to answer this).

  • What stage of moral development (according to Kohlberg) is exhibited by the people in the case study?

  • What stage is shown in your answers?

Prepare to discuss this case in front of the class. You will have five minutes to present and two minutes to answer questions. You must show a stakeholder map, identify the level of moral development, and explain your conclusion.
