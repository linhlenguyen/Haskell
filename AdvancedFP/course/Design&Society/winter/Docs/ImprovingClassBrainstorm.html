Results of the Improving Class Brainstorm

We brainstormed how we could improve the class this quarter. The class broke into 6 groups. Each group came up with 3-5 ideas and reported back to the class. The ideas were broken into two groups: 1) Things the staff could do, and 2) Things the students could do. Here is a compilation of the results.

Things the staff could do

  • Work more with computers, take advantage of technology.
  • Tie mentor session and class together more tightly.
  • More small group activities.
  • Assignments explained better.
  • Long range planning - Let everyone know when big projects are due.
  • Feedback on papers.
  • Snacks in class.
  • Extra Credit.
  • Divide time between students fairly in both class and mentor sessions.
  • Supply and use a grading rubric.
  • Choose more interesting books and readings.
  • Continue to give feedback on writings.
  • Study some poetry

Things the students could do

  • Bring required materials and assignments to class when they are due.
  • Listen more.
  • Speak up, ask clarifying questions, be more verbal.
  • Come to class prepared (especially the assigned readings).
  • Less smart-alec comments, be thoughtful.
