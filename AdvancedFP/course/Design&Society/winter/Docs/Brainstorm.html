Rules for Brainstorming

  • Postpone and withhold your judgment of ideas
  • Encourage wild and exaggerated ideas
  • Quantity counts at this stage, not quality
  • Build on the ideas put forward by others
  • Every person and every idea has equal worth

Process for Brain Storming

  • One person is the facilitator. She writes ideas on the whiteboard.
  • Participants call out new ideas
  • The facilitator writes down the ideas, she may not censor or alter ideas, only record them.
  • When the flow of new ideas ends, the group as a whole organizes the ideas.
  • This is the time for discussion
  • Several ideas my be the same, only in different words
  • Ideas may be grouped
