Montana Activity

In this activity each of the Collapse groups will pick one of the 9 sections of chapter 1 below.

  1. Montana's Economic History. pg. 27-mid 35
  2. Mining. pg. 35-top 41
  3. Forests. pg. 41-top 47
  4. Soil. pg. 47-49
  5. Water pg. 49-53
  6. Native & Non-native. pg. 53-56
  7. Differing Visions. pg. 56-lower 63
  8. Attitudes towards regulation. pg. 63-65
  9. 4 Stories. pg. 66-72

The group will discus the section, and then create and give a report to the rest of the class. Each report will have the following parts

  • A part that identifies the important messages (or key ideas) of that section.
  • A "Visual" that illustrates the key ideas (poster paper and markers provided). (Be sure each team members name appears on the visual).
  • A short presentation about the main points.
  • Answers questions from Tim, Liz and the Class about your section.
