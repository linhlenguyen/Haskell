Unlocking the clubhouse essay

Write a single paragraph (100-300 words) about an experience you had that influenced your perceptions, use, and attitudes about the use of computer technology.

I will grade this using the following rubric.

  • It is the correct length
  • It focusses on the assigned topic.
  • It follows the core rules of writing.
  • The paragraph has been proofread and edited.
  • You inform the reader about your own attitudes towards computer technology.
  • The grammar and spelling is correct.
