Library Hunt

Search the Miller Library

In your assigned team, use a catalogue terminal to guide your search for answers to worksheet questions. When you find an answer be sure to put the book or resource back in it's place on the shelf. The first team to complete the worksheet acuratly will recieve a prize, but cheaters will be disqualified.

As you scour the library keep in mind the research proposal due on Monday. What topic would you like to learn more about?

At after completing the search, return to the library lobby to turn in your completed library search worksheet.
