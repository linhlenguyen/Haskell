Dark Horse Celebration October 16
7pm
SMU Ballroom

The Portland State University Library invites you to the Dark Horse Celebration this Thursday, October 16 at 7pm in the Smith Student Union Ballroom (3rd fl). Mike Richardson, founder and president of Dark Horse Comics Inc., will talk about the history of comics and graphic novels.
