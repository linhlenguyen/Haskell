SHAC Walk-in Hours

A new drop-in program offered by the Center for Student Health and Counseling, "Let's Talk", offers walk-in, informal consultations, no appointment necessary, in Cramer 117, University Studies. Every Tuesday from 10:00am-1:00pm.

A CAPS counselor will be available to students and faculty for confidential consultations and referral.
