Collapse Chapter 2 Study Guide

Read "Collapse": Chapt. 1: Under Montanna's Big Sky (pp. 26 - 76)

Terms/Concepts/Ideas to Know

  • Where is Montana?
  • What is the geography of Montana?
  • The author describes Montana as a land of paradoxes. What does he mean by this? What is so paradoxical about Montana?
  • What were the three pillars of the Montana Economy in the past?
  • What drives the economy today?
  • What environmental problems plague Montana?
  • What social tensions exerbate Montana's physical problems?
  • How would you describe population growth in Montana?
  • Who are the people that tell their story in the chapter? What do they do for a living?
  • What is the Stock Farm and why is it significant?
  • What is salinization of soil, what are some things that might cause it, and why is it a problem?
