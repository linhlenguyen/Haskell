Lego Unit Overview

This document is an overview of the Lego Unit.

  • Notes
    1. Use of Gears
    2. Building Tips
    3. Motors and Sounds
    4. Sensors for Robotics
    5. Programming Robots
  • Number of activities
    1. Introduction and Practice
    2. Drag Race
    3. Tug of War
    4. Wallace and Grommet
    5. Project of your choice (theme: Assistive Technology)
  • Create and maintain a Team Blog.
    1. An entry for every day.
    2. Pictures of every activity or robot.
  • Final Project Writeup (with pictures)
  • Grading. Each Activity has a grading Rubric (See book).
    1. Often timed or competittion
    2. Must meet design criteria
    3. Bonus points to best design
  • Team evaluation. Each student must split 100 points amongst all the team members.
